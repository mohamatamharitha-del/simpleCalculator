Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the simpleCalculator Android application (hereby referred to as "the App") created by our developer team. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The App does NOT collect, store, or share any personal data or personally identifiable information. We do NOT send any data to external servers. All operations are performed locally on your device.

Microphone Permission

The App requires access to your device's Microphone (android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO) solely to enable the voice input feature for performing calculations. The audio data is processed locally on your device through the speech recognition service and is immediately discarded. The App does NOT store, transmit, or share your voice data.

Data Storage

All calculation history is stored locally within the App's secure database on your mobile device. You can search and view past calculations based on the calendar feature. This data never leaves your device and may be deleted at any time by clearing the App's storage in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

Children's Privacy

The App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13, as we do not collect any personal data from any users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Information

If you have any questions, suggestions, or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us at the developer email provided on the Google Play Store listing.
